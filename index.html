Josh Berson

Fiction, essay, noise. My work includes The Human Scaffold and The Meat Question, along with occasional pieces for venues ranging from Aeon to Res to Jane.

The still point at the center of my thinking is the relationship between skill and value — how our habits, inter alia, of listening, breathing, sitting, and eating inform what matters and conversely.

Represented by Salma Begum, Greyhound Literary.
